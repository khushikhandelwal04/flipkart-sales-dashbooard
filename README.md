# Flipkart E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard

A comprehensive data visualization dashboard analyzing Flipkart sales performance, logistics efficiency, and customer purchasing patterns over a key Q2 operational window. 

## 📊 Dashboard Preview

✨ **Interactive Visuals Include:**
* **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):** Real-time tracking of total gross sales volume.
* **Logistics Analysis:** Breakdowns of carrier performance, order statuses, and regional delivery distribution.
* **Product Insights:** Top-selling categories and item size preferences.

---

## 🚀 Key Insights & Metrics

### 💰 Financial Performance
* **Total Sales Amount:** 11.57M (Gross transaction volume recorded across the active date filter).

### 📦 Logistics & Courier Status
* **Shipped Orders:** Comprises the vast majority of transactions at **85.6%** (~17K orders).
* **On the Way:** **6.23%** (~1K orders) currently in transit.
* **Cancelled / Unshipped:** Combined total represents under **9%** of overall volume.
* **Delivery Partners:** **Ekart** handles the highest volume of orders, significantly outpacing **Easy Ship**.

### 👕 Product & Inventory Trends
* **Top Category:** **T-shirts** overwhelmingly dominate sales volume compared to Trousers, Wallets, Socks, and Shoes.
* **Size Demand:** Size **M (Medium)** is the highest-selling size, followed sequentially by **XL**, **XXL**, **S**, and **XS**.

  ### 🗺️ Geographical Distribution
* **Top States:** **Maharashtra** generates the highest order volume, followed closely by **Karnataka** and **Telangana**.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Features

* **Data Visualization:** Power BI / Tableau Desktop
* **Data Fields Tracked:** Courier Status, Delivery Partner, Fulfilment Channel, Product Size, Category, and Shipping State.
* **Time-Series Filtering:** Dynamic date slider allowing deep dives into custom date ranges (e.g., 18-04-2022 to 30-04-2022).
